2. Pass the “narrow” Width on Fashion Pages
In UniversalCategoryPage.tsx (and similarly in SimpleCategoryPage.tsx), adjust the <EnterprisePageLayout> invocation:

-  />
+  <EnterprisePageLayout
+    leftSidebar={leftSidebar}
+    mainContent={mainContent}
+    rightSidebar={rightSidebar}
+    // Shrink the right sidebar on fashion pages to free up ~232px
+    rightSidebarWidth={category === 'fashion' ? 'narrow' : 'standard'}
+    dynamicPadding={dynamicSpacing}
+  />
With these two isolated changes:

Standard pages keep the full 320 px right sidebar (no visible change).

Fashion pages now use a narrow 88 px sidebar, freeing up ~232 px. Combined with px-0 padding, your main grid goes from 704 px → 936 px (≈234 px per product minus gaps), which is very close to the 248 px target—if you need the last 16 px you can fine-tune the sidebar or padding further.
